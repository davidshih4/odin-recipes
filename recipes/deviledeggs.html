Breakfast Burrito

Delicious deviled eggs!

Ingredients

  • 12 eggs
  • mayo
  • Salt, pepper, cayane pepper

Steps

  1. Hard boil all the eggs
  2. Put hard boiled eggs in ice bath to rest
  3. Peel, cut in half, put egg yolks into bowl
  4. Mix mayo, salt, and pepper with the egg yolks
  5. Add a heaping dollop to each half egg
  6. Sprinkle cayane pepper and enjoy!
